Named for the Best Friend I Ever Had

Lyka herself — always keeping watch, never alone.

For sixteen years, a dog named Lyka gave the kind of friendship most people only read about — patient, loyal, and completely unconditional. Lyka's Pantry exists to pass that love along: honest treats, baked by hand in small batches, made only with ingredients we'd eat ourselves.

Every batch is made the way I would have made it for her. — Bucky, founder
